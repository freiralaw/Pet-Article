Fun facts

A group of cats is called a clowder, a male cat is called a tom, a female cat is called a molly or queen while young cats are called kittens.

Rabbits bond closely with their owners. Ask any bunny owner who interacts regularly with his pet and he'll tell you that, just like dogs or cats, rabbits get to know their owners well.

Many turtle species (not all) can hide their heads inside their shells when attacked by predators. They also have an upper shell called 'carapace'

-Parrots are among the most intelligent birds.
-Parrots are the only birds that can eat with their feet.
-Some parrots can live for over 80 years.

-Fish have gills that extract oxygen from the water around them.
-There are over 30000 known species of fish.
-Some flatfish use camouflage to hide themselves on the ocean floor.
